Lithium-ion battery stacks

  • Use case: Renewable energy buffering
  • Rather expensive but high energy capacity
  • Uptime and reliability are most important
  • Maintaining battery health is an issue!

The solution

  • Monitor every cell
  • Keep voltage & temperature within safe limit
  • Replace failed or old batteries
  • ...all without taking the system down!
